About Me

I took computer engineering course in college hoping that I will be able to create games or do something useful, but after graduating It really is the start of searching my life worth or what they call pursuit of happiness, struggle is real, specially for a person like me who doesn't really know anything in manila. I grew in Nueva Ecija province. First I look for Network Admin or Jr Dev but no luck for me, so there is this QA or Quality Assurance Engineer position came up, with only limited budget and a month of hoping to land a job I took it without hesitation. After that there are opportunities, I don't know but I hesitate to grab it thinking that maybe this is not for me. But after that opportunities still came then I realize maybe God throwing it to my face without knowing. So I started saving and look for bootcamps also consider enrolling on 6 months course, but no luck cause bootcamp cost in manila will never be affordable for me. But one day Tuitt came and their payment scheme really suit me. 3 months extensive training with them boost my self-esteem, Tuitt really bridge the gap on getting your dream job, and now I can say that I am a Web Developer.
